Frequently Asked Questions

If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please contact our live support service.

You can book an appointment online via our website's appointment system. You can also call our hospital to make an appointment by phone.

You can cancel your appointment by logging into your account on our website or by calling our call center.

A standard appointment takes approximately 15 minutes. However, this may vary depending on your doctor's schedule.

You can find detailed address information and our location map on our Contact page.

You can visit the MEDICAL DEPARTMENTS page from the top menu of our website to see the departments we serve.

To log into our system, you can use your username and password on our website.

You can send your suggestions and complaints by filling out the form on our Contact page or by calling our call center.
