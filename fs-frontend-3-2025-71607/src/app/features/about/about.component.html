About Us

Pair 5 Hospital, founded in 1948 at its current location on Bülten Street, Kavaklıdere, is the first private hospital in Turkey.

Since its establishment, the hospital has provided services in multiple departments. Today, our hospital offers care in Orthopedics and Traumatology, Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation, Neurosurgery, General Surgery, ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat), Urology, Internal Medicine, Cardiology, Neurology, Pulmonology, Anesthesiology and Reanimation, Nutrition and Dietetics, Radiology, and all Laboratory Services.

In recent years, with significant infrastructure and medical technology investments, combined with our deep-rooted experience, Pair 5 Hospital has strengthened its reliability and leadership in the healthcare services it provides.

Our Vision

Our hospital's vision is to protect public health and build a healthier future. We aim to improve and preserve the lives of our patients by delivering quality healthcare services and staying up to date with modern medical technologies and advancements.

We prioritize respect, compassion, and justice by preparing personalized treatment plans for each patient. Supporting the career goals and continuous development of our healthcare professionals is one of our main commitments through training and development programs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to protect the health of the community and work towards a healthier future. We aim to enhance and maintain the quality of life of our patients by delivering top-tier healthcare services while adopting the latest medical technologies and practices.

We are always by our patients' side, whether in health or sickness, developing personalized treatment plans based on respect, compassion, and fairness. We strive to support the professional growth and development of our healthcare staff through continuous learning opportunities.

Patient satisfaction and safety are our highest priorities. We apply medical ethics in all our services, aiming to provide the best possible care. We also raise awareness through health education, empowering our patients to live healthier lives.

As a leading institution in healthcare services, our hospital is committed to meeting the health needs of society and contributing to a healthier future. With a belief in "healthy life, peaceful future", we deliver healthcare services that truly make a difference.
